Introduction

I see you are interested in my novel, but before we wonder into the land of fantasy I would like you to know that every statement, event, place, culture, quotes, saying e.t.c are just fiction.

So, I wouldn't want anyone imitating anything that was written here or acting based on a saying in this novel. Please read this novel with the intention of getting entertained alone, don't expect any knowledge about anything from it, thank you and enjoy.

Also, please note that there won't be a character page where a character's detail is written. You will come to know characters as they appear somewhere along the storyline and you get to decide how the characters seem to you.

Furthermore, I might describe most races, creatures, things, or festivals differently than they are, and than they are described in myths.

I hope to broaden the scope of your imagination in a positive way, but let's keep it all in our imagination shall we?, thank you. With no further ado we are going to enter the broad world of Asfureruta.
